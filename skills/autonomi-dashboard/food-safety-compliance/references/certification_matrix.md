## 2. SQF Level 3 — Details

### 2.1 What SQF Level 3 Covers
SQF Level 3 is the highest tier of the Safe Quality Food scheme and covers both food safety AND product quality. It requires:
- A documented and implemented food safety management system (FSMS)
- A documented quality management system aligned to the SQF Code
- Third-party certification audit by an SQF Licensed Certification Body
- Annual recertification audit
- Unannounced audit option (taken by PurePress — qualifies for premium retailer programs)

### 2.2 SQF System Elements Required at Level 3

| SQF Element | Description | Autonomi Coverage |
|---|---|---|
| 2.1 Management Commitment | Food safety policy, responsibilities | Dashboard governance + Weston sign-off |
| 2.2 Document Control | Document management system | Compliance page document library |
| 2.3 Specifications | Ingredient, packaging, finished product specs | BOM specs + COA library |
| 2.4 Achieving Food Safety | HACCP plan | 7 CCPs documented in AGNT-09 |
| 2.5 SQF System Verification | Internal audits, management review | AGNT-11 CAPA + audit scheduler |
| 2.6 Product Identification, Trace, Withdrawal | Lot traceability + recall | AGNT-13 FSMA 204 + AGNT-14 recall |
| 2.7 Food Defense | Food defense plan | On file; updated annually |
| 2.8 Food Fraud | Vulnerability assessment | AGNT-27 supplier risk assessment |
| 11 (Beverage Module) | Product-specific requirements for beverages | HACCP + process controls |

## 4. FSSC 22000 Version 6.0 — Details

### 4.1 Structure
FSSC 22000 is a GFSI-benchmarked scheme built on:
- ISO 22000:2018 (Food Safety Management Systems)
- ISO/TS 22002-1:2009 (Prerequisite programs for food manufacturing)
- FSSC 22000 Additional Requirements (v6.0)

### 4.2 Key v6.0 Additional Requirements

| Section | Requirement | Autonomi Implementation |
|---|---|---|
| 2.5.1 | Food Defense | Documented food defense plan; threat assessment updated annually |
| 2.5.2 | Food Fraud | Vulnerability assessment covering 100% of direct ingredients |
| 2.5.4 | Allergen Management | Full allergen program; AGNT-10 automated control |
| 2.5.5 | Environmental Monitoring | Zone 1-4 EMP; Listeria indicator testing |
| 2.5.6 | Product Development | Hazard analysis for all new products before commercial release |
| 2.5.14 | AI and Automation | Documented AI governance policy covering all 29 agents |

**Note on 2.5.14:** This is a v6.0 addition specifically relevant to Autonomi. The AI governance policy documents how each agent's decisions are validated, audited, and overridden when needed. Blockchain records serve as the verification mechanism.

### 4.3 Certification Cycle
- Year 1: Full certification audit (4 days; 2 auditors)
- Year 2: Surveillance audit 1 (2 days)
- Year 3: Surveillance audit 2 + recertification (3 days)
